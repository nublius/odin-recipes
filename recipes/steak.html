Steak

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Olive Oil
  • Cloves of Garlic
  • Herbs
  • Dry Red Wine
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Place steak inside a resealable bag. Stir olive oil, garlic, parsley, rosemary, chives, red wine, salt, pepper, and mustard powder together in a small bowl
  2. Pour marinade over steak in bag. Press out as much air and seal bag. Refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours
  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Sear and cook steak for 3 to 4 minutes on each side
  4. Discard marinade and allow steak to rest for about 5 minutes before serving
