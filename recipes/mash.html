Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine potatoes, garlic, and salt in saucepan with water
  3. Bring to boil over high, and then reduce to a medium-low simmer for 10 to 12 minutes
  4. Drain potatoes and return to saucepan
  5. Add butter and mash until smooth
  6. Add seasonings and transfer to a serving bowl
